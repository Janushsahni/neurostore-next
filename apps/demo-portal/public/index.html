Client-Side Encryption • AI Placement • Failover Retrieval

Server never sees plaintext. Users upload from browser only.

Neurostore encrypts files in-browser, distributes encrypted shards to independent nodes, verifies storage receipts, and retrieves from surviving nodes during outages.
